Cavi Lipo FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Cavi Lipo

Questions

How many sessions are needed to see results?

You may see results after your first session, with more improvement noticed within a few days. However, 3 sessions are strongly recommended for optimal results; 3+ sessions for problem areas. How often can you get Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Treatments? Cavi-Lipo Treatments can be performed every 4 days.

How long are the sessions?

Approximately 30-45 minutes.

Why is Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation different from other treatments?

Laser technology and cold technology are temporary solutions as they do eliminate the fat but unfortunately leave the fat cell intact. The issue is that the fat cell remains and can later be filled again with fat. The other issue is that these machines do not have an effect directly on tightening the skin. Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation not only works on the fat but also the fat cell! Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation is the closest we can get to liposuction without surgery. The cavitation process causes a “bubbling” of the fat cell to the point it literally pops or explodes and both the fat and the cell are permanently removed by the body as waste. Once the fat cell is GONE you have permanent, instant changes. With Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation you will achieve faster, slimming results and also stimulate collagen, yielding skin tightening and toning.

Are the results long-term?

The Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation system destroys the fat cell. Results can be maintained when combined with exercise and proper nutrition. Cavi-Lipo does not stop natural aging that affects your appearance. For this reason, maintenance visits are recommended.

What results can I expect?

The main goal of Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation therapy is body shaping and reducing stubborn, fatty areas that don't seem to respond to diet or exercise. It can also be used for skin tightening. Following After Care instructions will ensure best results.

Who would benefit from Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Therapy?

The treatment is best suited for the average to moderately overweight person who has a healthy lifestyle and exercises regularly. The Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation is not a miracle device that will strip away huge layers of built up fat in a single session. It is ideal for people with isolated areas of extra fat. It is perfect for those trying to avoid surgery and those who are looking for modest reductions of fat in problem areas.

Who should not receive Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Therapy?

You may not be able to participate in the Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Therapy if you have the following contraindications: Pregnant, Breast-Feeding, or Undergoing FertilityTreatments; Heart disease, High or Low Blood Pressure, Circulation Problems, Arrhythmias, Irregular Heart Rhythm; Pacemaker or Metal Hardware; Compromised Immune System; Cancer; Liver or Kidney Disease; Long Term Use of: Anti-Inflammatory, Anti-Coagulants, Antibiotics; Skin Problems; Under the Age of 18. Please contact the office or your medical provider to discuss your eligibility for this program.

What will I feel?

The procedure is painless, but you may hear a noise from the ultrasound head applicator. Manual Lymph Drainage techniques and Massage Therapy will feel very relaxing.

How safe is it?

Studies have shown that Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation Treatment is safe, however the ultrasound procedure should not be used on clients with any contraindications listed above.

How do I achieve maximum results?

Follow our After Care instructions for optimal results. Drinking water, light to brisk exercise, depending on your health, and a balanced diet are all integral parts of this program. Refraining from alcohol during treatment will also be recommended.

What is the Biggest risk?

CAUTION: Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation Treatment plus US combined with nutrition and moderate exercise is so effective you may need to purchase a new wardrobe!!!
