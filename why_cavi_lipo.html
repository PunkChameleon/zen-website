why cavi lipo?

FDA approved technology, Cavi-Lipo Ultrasound Cavitation and our additional techniques have proven to be a terrific, non-invasive liposuction alternative. This Ultrasound approach is a non-invasive painless session, unlike other treatments on the market today such as Liposuction, Smart Lipo, Laser Lipo, Liposonix, Coolsculpting, Velashape, Zerona, Vasorshape and many others. Cavi-Lipo is entirely pain free and gives lasting results without the use of suction, freezing, pulling, bruising, burning, surgery or side effects. With no down time, pain, side effects or expensive commitments, we will permanently destroy fat, in addition to tightening the skin.

Look as good on the outside as you do on the inside!

Initial Visit includes visual consultation, before photos (your discretion) and measurements, program overview and of course, Cavi-Lipo ultrasound cavitation. Subsequent sessions will also include manual lymph drainage techniques, additional massage time (time permitting) and it's the perfect time for quiet time and maybe even a nap!

Each session takes approximately 30-45 minutes.

An area is approximately the same size as an 8.5 X 11 sheet of paper, however if your body responds well, it could be double that. Everybody is unique, therefore results and the amount of sessions needed will vary by individual.

3 sessions are strongly recommended, and 3+ sessions for problem areas.
